Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 c sugar
  • 1/2 c margarine or butter softened
  • 1/2 c shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 2-3/4 c flour
  • 2 t cream of tartar
  • 1 t baking soda
  • 1/4 c sugar
  • 2 t ground cinnamon

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400.

Mix 1 1/2 cups sugar, the butter, shortening and eggs in a large bowl. Stir in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt.

Shape dough into balls. Mix 1/4 cup sugar and the cinnamon. Roll balls in cinnamon-sugar mixture. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake 8 to 10 minutes. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.
